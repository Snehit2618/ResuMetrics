Welcome to Resume Analyzer

Welcome to the Resume Analyzer Tool! Our platform helps job seekers enhance their resumes to increase their chances of landing the perfect job. Whether you're an employee looking to improve your resume or a recruiter looking to streamline the hiring process, our tool is here to help!

For Employees: You can upload your resume and get personalized feedback on areas such as skills, experience, and overall presentation. Our AI-powered analysis will suggest improvements to make your resume stand out to recruiters.

For Recruiters: Our tool also allows you to analyze multiple resumes at once, compare candidates' qualifications, and predict how well they might fit a specific job role based on your criteria. Streamline your hiring process with automated resume evaluations and make informed decisions faster.
